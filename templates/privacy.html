Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the expense tracking features of Spendly — displaying your records, generating summaries, and enabling filters. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access. However, no method of storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third parties. We may use infrastructure providers to host the Service, but these providers do not have access to your expense data beyond what is required to operate the hosting environment.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to request deletion of your data, please reach out to us. We will respond to all reasonable requests within a reasonable timeframe.
